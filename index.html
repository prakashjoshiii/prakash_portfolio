About Me

I'm a Geomatics Engineering student at Kathmandu University with a passion for transforming spatial data into actionable insights. My expertise lies in UAV-based remote sensing, GIS development, and spatial database management. I've led projects ranging from NDVI-based crop yield estimation to urban heat island analysis using Google Earth Engine. Proficient in tools like QGIS, PostGIS, and Python, I aim to bridge the gap between geospatial technology and real-world applications.

  • Urban Heat Island Dynamics — 20-year UHI study using Google Earth Engine.
  • Hydropower Feasibility Survey — Route analysis using RTK GNSS and Total Station.
  • Route Survey & Geometric Design — Road alignment design and elevation profiling.
  • Spatial DBMS Routing — PgRouting-based shortest path with PostGIS & QGIS.
